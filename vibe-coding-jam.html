AGI House Event Recap

Vibe Coding Jam & Tea

Date January 25, 2026
Time 3:00 PM – 7:00 PM PST
Location AGI House, San Francisco
Technology is no longer a moat, but taste is.

An afternoon of tea, conversation, and making. Each participant arrived with an idea and left with something they had built.

The Hosts

Crystal Shan, ShanShan & Qinyu

Product designers from Meta, TikTok, and Cisco. They introduced YAM, a design tool that considers the thinking behind a product, not just its appearance.

In partnership with AGI House SF, Jeremy Nixon & Julius Ritter

What Happened

  • YAM: Participants used the tool to move from concept to finished design.
  • Open mic: Builders shared their approaches to making software with consideration.
  • Build session: Everyone made something and shipped it before leaving.
  • Tea: Carefully prepared. Essential to the atmosphere.

The Tea

Jasmine. Rose. Hibiscus. Chamomile. The selection was deliberate.
