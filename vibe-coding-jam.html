AGI House Event Recap

Vibe Coding Jam & Tea

Date January 25, 2026
Time 3:00 PM – 7:00 PM PST
Location AGI House, San Francisco
Technology is no longer a moat, but taste is.

On a Saturday afternoon in January, vibe builders gathered at AGI House to sip tea, share ideas, and craft products with intention. This wasn't a typical hackathon—it was a celebration of taste, design, and the belief that beautiful, thoughtful experiences can change how people interact with the world.

The Hosts

Crystal Shan, ShanShan & Qinyu

Product design founders from Meta, TikTok, and Cisco, with deep roots in human–computer interaction and product craft. They brought their creation—YAM, the next-gen vibe design copilot—for participants to explore.

In partnership with AGI House SF, Jeremy Nixon & Julius Ritter

What Happened

  • Hands-on time with YAM — Participants explored the next-gen vibe design copilot, going from strategy to polished product. YAM doesn't just generate screens—it helps you think: shaping the vision, developing strategy, exploring solutions, and executing with taste and craft.
  • A community of vibe builders connected over shared values of taste and impact
  • Tea, snacks, and deep conversations about how to win users' hearts through intentional design
  • Open mic session where builders shared how they vibe code to infuse their unique taste
  • Build session where every participant shipped a product reflecting their authentic vision

The Tea

A curated selection of loose-leaf teas set the tone—jasmine, rose, hibiscus, chamomile, and more—each carefully chosen to inspire creativity and contemplation.
