Virtual Cell Challenge: Bio x AI Hackathon

Sunday, September 21, 2025

11:00am – 11:00pm
170 St Germain Ave, San Francisco, CA
Hosted by AGI House

Come train virtual cell models with us!! Come take your place in the conquest of bio by artificial intelligence. GPUs are yours from Hyperbolic. Virtual cell data is thoughtfully offered by the Arc Institute, and their folk will be coming to help you along.

Virtual cells could eliminate the trillion-dollar trial-and-error pharmaceutical industry by allowing scientists to test millions of drug combinations instantly across every known human genetic variant, potentially curing diseases like Alzheimer's and cancer within a decade rather than centuries.

By simulating how cellular aging occurs at the molecular level, these digital twins of our biology could reveal the precise interventions needed to extend healthy human lifespan to 150 years while maintaining the physical capabilities of a 30-year-old.

Virtual cells could enable us to design entirely new forms of life—engineering bacteria that consume plastic pollution and excrete valuable materials, or creating cellular machines that repair radiation damage in astronauts during multi-generational journeys to distant stars.
