About

A Phenomenal Gathering

"A Manifold Conference with all past guests on the podcast as a phenomenal gathering of this generation of edgy scientific talent."

For five years, the Manifold podcast has brought together the world's most interesting minds to explore questions at the frontier of human knowledge—from theoretical physics to evolutionary psychology, geopolitics to artificial intelligence, science fiction to high finance.

Now, for the first time, we're bringing these conversations into the physical world. A two-track conference where every attendee presents their most interesting ideas, combined with intimate dinners and a daily summit walk to Twin Peaks.

This is not a typical conference. There are no sponsors, no booths, no networking. Just the most interesting people you've never met, gathered in one of the most beautiful places on Earth. Flying in makes sense.

Anywhere from 50 to 100 folk. Set up both the basement and mid floor for presentation, cozy conversations. Each day ends with a collective summit walk to the top of Twin Peaks.

Experience

What to Expect

Two-Track Presentations

Parallel sessions on two floors. "The Frontier" covers cutting-edge science and technology. "The World" explores geopolitics, economics, and society.

Cozy Conversations

Intimate fireside chats, small group discussions, and impromptu dialogues. Every attendee brings their most interesting idea to share.

Summit Walks

Each day concludes with a collective walk to the top of Twin Peaks. Conversations continue as the city lights emerge below.

Curated Meals

Lunch and dinner served family-style. Seating arranged to maximize unexpected connections.

Lightning Talks

Five-minute presentations on radical ideas. No slides, no preparation. Just the most interesting thing you've learned recently.

Unexpected Moments

Late-night jam sessions, spontaneous debates, secret speakers. The best moments can't be planned.
