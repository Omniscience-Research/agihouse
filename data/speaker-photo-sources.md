## Quick Download Tips

1. **Twitter photos**: Right-click profile pic → "Copy image address" → download
2. **LinkedIn**: View profile → click photo → right-click → save
3. **Company sites**: Usually in /about or /team pages
4. **Recommended size**: 400x400px minimum, square crop, JPG format
