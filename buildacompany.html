AGI House SF Presents

Build A Company

A 48-hour hackathon to build real businesses. Revenue traction wins the Grand Prize — with additional categories for creativity, consumer appeal, and unicorn potential, judged by a panel of operators and builders.

Dates
April 24 – 26, 2026
Venue
AGI House SF
Format
48 hours · in person
The Premise

Not Your Normal Hackathon

Ever dreamed of building a startup in a weekend? This is your chance. The Build-a-Company-a-Thon isn't about prototypes — it's about launching real businesses that generate actual revenue in 48 hours.

The Rules

This isn't your typical hackathon where polished demos win prizes. The Grand Prize goes to revenue traction — but our judges also award meaningful prizes for product quality, creativity, consumer appeal, and unicorn potential.

Real customers. Real money. Real business. Come ready to ideate, build, launch, and sell.

What To Bring

Bring your skills in product development, growth hacking, sales, and everything in between. Bring your best business ideas.

We'll provide the space, the mentors, the food, and everything else you need to build a company in 48 hours.

I.

Ideate

Form your team. Find your wedge. Pick something the market actually wants.

II.

Build & Launch

Ship a real product. Put it in front of real users. Take real payments.

III.

Sell

Revenue wins the Grand Prize. Craft, creativity, and ambition win the rest.

The Weekend

Schedule

Forty-eight hours from the opening bell to the final tally of revenue.

Friday

Opening Remarks, Team Formation & Hacking Begins

Kickoff at AGI House SF. Partner intros, team formation, and the starting whistle.

Saturday

Hacking Continues All Day

Mentors on-site, breakfast and dinner provided. Build, launch, sell.

Sunday

Finalizing Projects & Judging

Last push for revenue. Judges review submissions. Prizes awarded.

The Spoils

Prize Categories

A revenue-based Grand Prize alongside three judged categories evaluating technical execution, product quality, and market ambition.

Grand Prize

Most Revenue Generated

The team with the highest verifiable revenue at the end of 48 hours. Real customers. Real money. The market decides.

I

Best Consumer App

For the team that built the most compelling product for everyday users — judged on product quality, polish, and consumer appeal.

II

Most Creative Product

For the team with the most original idea, execution, or angle — judged on inventiveness and originality of approach.

III

Greatest Unicorn Potential

For the team most likely to become a billion-dollar company — judged on market size, defensibility, and long-term ambition.

Tandem

AI Onboarding for SaaS

Tandem drops an AI agent into any SaaS product to get new users to their "aha moment" fast. Instead of generic product tours and FAQ pages, Tandem's agent reads the page, understands context, and guides users step by step — like a CS rep who's always in the room.

The Panel

Judges

Our judging panel was curated and invitation-only — selected for demonstrated technical expertise and a track record of building production-grade AI systems. Judges evaluated submissions on technical execution, product quality, customer value, go-to-market thinking, and long-term business potential, and their votes determined the Best Consumer App, Most Creative Product, and Greatest Unicorn Potential prize categories. They served alongside engineers and researchers from OpenAI, Qdrant, and Tandem, chosen on comparable industry standing.
